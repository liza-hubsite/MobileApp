Inside your body

Day 3 is still part of the menstrual phase. The uterus is shedding its lining, hormone levels are low, and you may feel lower energy or have mild cramps. At the same time, your body is already preparing for the next cycle by starting to mature new eggs in the ovaries. Based on your previous ovulation history, ovulation is expected in 11 days.

Day 3 Action Plan

Low-Impact Movement

Gentle movement like light yoga, stretching, or short walks can help soothe lower back discomfort and release tension. These activities may support flexibility, circulation, and relaxation.

Iron-Rich Nutrition

Eating iron-rich foods supports healthy red blood cells and energy. Good sources include meat, beans, lentils, and leafy greens. Pair with vitamin C foods to improve absorption.
